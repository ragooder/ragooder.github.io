Dependencies: 2 Segments

Imagine that you have a Prospect with 2 Segments (Segment A & Segment B).
The outcome of Segment A may affect the Chance of Success for Segment B.

Use the sliders to edit the Chance of Success (CoS) and Mean volume for each segment and the dependency between them:
A Positive dependency means if Segment A succeeds then Segment B is more likely to succeed,
A Negative dependency means if Segment A succeeds then Segment B is more likely to fail.
